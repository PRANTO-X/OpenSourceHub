About OpenSourceHub

We're on a mission to connect developers with amazing open source projects and foster a global community of collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

Our Mission

OpenSourceHub exists to democratize access to open source software, making it easier for developers to discover, contribute to, and learn from projects that are shaping the future of technology.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every developer can easily find and contribute to open source projects that align with their interests and skills, accelerating innovation and collaboration across the globe.

Our Purpose

To build the most comprehensive and user-friendly platform for discovering open source projects, while fostering a supportive community that celebrates contribution and shared knowledge.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Quality Over Quantity

We carefully curate projects to ensure they meet high standards of quality, documentation, and maintenance.

Community Driven

Our platform is built by and for the open source community. We value feedback, contributions, and collaboration.

Openness & Transparency

We believe in open processes, transparent decision-making, and sharing our own journey as we build this platform.
