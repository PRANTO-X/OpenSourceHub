Share your open source project with our community of developers. Get feedback, contributors, and recognition for your work.

Submission Guidelines

  • Project must be open source with a clear license
  • Provide a clear description and project goals
  • Include relevant tags for better discovery
  • GitHub repository must be publicly accessible
  • Project should have some level of activity
  • No spam, advertising, or malicious projects

Review Process

Projects are reviewed within 48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your project is published or if we need more information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about submitting projects to OpenSourceHub

How long does review take?

Projects are typically reviewed within 48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your project is published or if we need more information.

What makes a good project description?

A good description clearly explains what your project does, its purpose, key features, and technologies used. It should be concise but informative.

Can I update my project after submission?

Yes, you can update your project information at any time after it's published. Simply visit your project page and click the "Edit" button.

What if my project is rejected?

If your project doesn't meet our guidelines, we'll provide feedback on what needs to be improved. You can resubmit after making the necessary changes.
